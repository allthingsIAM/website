About Me

Hi, I'm Andrew.

I'm Andrew Chanthaphone — an IAM practitioner, educator, and LinkedIn Learning instructor on a mission to make Identity & Access Management approachable and career-changing for everyone.

Over my career I've worked across business analyst, project manager, and director-level IAM roles, helping organizations solve complex identity challenges in both the federal and commercial sectors. I specialize in Microsoft Entra ID, Okta, and SailPoint, and I've led large-scale tenant-to-tenant migrations in highly regulated environments.

I've spoken at Identiverse and Cyberjutsu Con, appeared on podcasts like Identity at the Center, and built this community because I believe IAM is one of the most important — and underappreciated — disciplines in cybersecurity today.

When I'm not creating YouTube videos or course content, I'm hanging out with my family, hitting the gym, or cheering (sometimes booing) my favorite sports teams.
